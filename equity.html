Fractional Shares — What to Know (Placeholder)

🧩 Fractional shares reference

• What fractional shares are — buy portions of shares instead of whole ones
• Why they matter — access high-priced names (BRK.B, GOOGL) with any $ amount
• Broker differences — Fidelity supports · IBKR supports · Tastytrade limited
• Dividend treatment — proportional dividend on fractional holdings
• Tax implications — no different from whole shares
• Limitations — some brokers only allow market orders on fractionals
