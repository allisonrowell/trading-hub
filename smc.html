The 3 Core Rules To Remember Always
memorize
RuleWhy It Matters
1. Only trade Discount zone (longs) or Premium zone (shorts)
Equilibrium trades have no statistical edge. Skip them, even when tempting. This eliminates ~70% of bad setups.
2. SmartTrend Score ≥ 75 = take, < 65 = skip
The Score is the gatekeeper. The flashy "Strong Buy/Sell" labels can fire on bad setups — the Score tells the truth.
3. Scale 1/3 at first resistance, ALWAYS
The most common way profitable trades become losers: holding through obvious resistance. Take partial profits without exception.
If you remember nothing else: These 3 rules alone will keep you out of 80% of losing trades and protect 80% of winning trades. Everything else in this hub is detail on top of these three foundations.

Strong / Weak High & Low — Bias Prediction
read first
LabelMeaningImplication
Strong High
Swing high that rejected sharply AND broke the prior swing low. Sellers proved control here.
Expected to HOLD
Weak High
Swing high that pulled back shallow without breaking prior swing low. Sellers weren't committed.
Expected to BREAK
Strong Low
Swing low that bounced sharply AND broke the prior swing high. Buyers proved control here.
Expected to HOLD
Weak Low
Swing low that bounced shallow without breaking prior swing high. Buyers weren't committed.
Expected to BREAK
The 4 combinations:
• Strong High + Weak Low = Bearish bias (sellers control, low will break)
• Weak High + Strong Low = Bullish bias (buyers control, high will break)
• Strong + Strong = Range-bound (mean reversion plays)
• Weak + Weak = Transitional / unclear (wait for one to break first)

The Swing Trading Workflow — Top-Down Analysis
execute in order
Step 1
Daily Chart
Identify SWING TREND. Strong/Weak labels? Last BOS or CHoCH direction? Where in the range?
Step 2
Find The Zone
Bullish trend → bullish swing OB in Discount. Bearish → bearish OB in Premium. Confluence with weekly level.
Step 3
4H Chart
When price enters Daily zone, drop to 4H. Look for CHoCH in direction of Daily bias.
Step 4
1H Chart
Confirm 4H CHoCH on 1H close. Volume picking up. MACD agreeing.
Step 5
Execute
Enter ITM option 30-90 DTE. Stop below Daily OB. Targets at next Daily swing high/low or Strong High/Low.
The key insight: Higher timeframe sets DIRECTION. Lower timeframe sets TIMING. Never enter on 1H alone — Daily must agree first. Never wait for 1H confirmation on a Daily setup — you'll miss the move.

Rule of thumb: Buy 2-3× more TIME than you think you need. Theta is the swing trader's enemy. If you think the move takes 2 weeks, buy 6 weeks of expiration. The extra premium is INSURANCE against being right on direction but wrong on timing.
Avoid OTM calls/puts on swing trades. They look "cheap" but theta destroys them while you wait days for the move to play out. Pay up for ITM — the higher delta means the option moves dollar-for-dollar with the underlying once your thesis works.
